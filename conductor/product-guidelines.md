# Product Guidelines

## Tone & Voice
The system's interactions should strike a balance between being a supportive partner and a rigorous logician.
- **Empathetic & Collaborative:** Act as a supportive partner that understands the user's challenges.
- **Direct & Objective:** Focus on efficiency and logical clarity, presenting facts without fluff.
- **Inquisitive & Systematic:** Use the 5 Whys technique to encourage deeper root cause analysis and critical thinking.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity:** Prioritize clear logic and transparent processes over unnecessary features.
- **Minimal Friction:** Minimize the number of steps and cognitive load for the user during the inquiry process.
- **Feedback-Driven Guidance:** Provide constant validation and progress updates to keep the user informed.

## Visual & Branding Style
- **Clean & Minimalist:** A professional look and feel that emphasizes typography, white space, and content clarity. Avoid unnecessary visual distractions.

## Communication & Presentation
- **Simple & Jargon-Free:** Use plain language that is accessible to all users, regardless of their technical background.
- **Precise & Professional:** Employ accurate technical and logical terminology when necessary to maintain rigor.
- **Transparent Reasoning:** Always explain the "why" behind every question or conclusion to build trust and understanding.
